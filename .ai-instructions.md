# Nakama Project Constitution (2026)

## 1. Networking Hierarchy
- **P2P Priority:** Comms must prioritize direct P2P connections (Local/NAN) over Relay (TURN). 
- **Diversity Mode:** Utilize Wi-Fi 7 MLO to send redundant voice packets over 2.4GHz and 5GHz simultaneously. If one link fails in a high-interference gym environment, the other must sustain the stream without reset.
- **Tunneling:** The app must support Tailscale integration to reach the home server when not on the local SSID.

## 2. Audio Engine Laws
- **The Ducking Rule:** When the PTT (Push-To-Talk) is active or VAD (Voice Activity Detection) triggers, the Music lane must attenuate to 20% volume instantly. 
- **Caching:** Implement a 5-song "Lookahead Cache" to ensure the music never stops during gym-WiFi dead zones or handoffs.

## 3. OS Persistence
- **Android:** Use a 'Foreground Service' to prevent the OS from killing the P2P radio link.
- **iOS:** Integrate 'CallKit' to maintain microphone access and audio focus while the screen is locked and the phone is in a pocket.

## 4. UX Strategy
- **Gym-Native UI:** Elegant but not overly complicated. Make sure parts of the UI are easily accessible while the user is working out.
- **Signal Insight:** Provide a 'Diversity Status' overlay showing the health of both the 2.4GHz and 5GHz lanes.
